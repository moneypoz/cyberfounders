## Overview

Sonder Security was founded to address a structural problem in enterprise security: the perpetual friction between security and development teams. Rather than selling a product, they embed as advisors to transform how organizations build and ship software securely.

## What They're Building

Their practice focuses on DevSecOps maturity — helping CISOs and engineering leaders establish shift-left security practices, threat modeling pipelines, and shared accountability models. Clients include major public organizations navigating digital transformation under compliance pressure.

## Why It Matters

The average enterprise has hundreds of developers and a handful of security engineers. Security can't scale as a gate; it has to scale as a culture. Sonder's model trains organizations to make that cultural shift stick.
