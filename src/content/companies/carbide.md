## Overview

Carbide, formerly known as Securicy, emerged from a simple observation: the tools that help enterprises manage compliance were never designed for companies with 20 engineers and one part-time security person. Carbide rebuilds that workflow from scratch for high-growth startups and scale-ups.

## What They're Building

The platform covers the full compliance lifecycle — policy creation, employee training, evidence collection, continuous control monitoring, and audit preparation — across the major frameworks. A key differentiator is their emphasis on turning compliance into an ongoing posture rather than a one-time certification sprint.

## Why It Matters

SOC 2 and ISO 27001 have become table stakes for B2B SaaS companies closing enterprise deals. Carbide lowers the cost and time-to-certification without sacrificing the depth that real enterprise buyers require during security reviews.
