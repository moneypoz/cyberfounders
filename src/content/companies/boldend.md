## Overview

Boldend operated at the sharp end of cybersecurity — building tools and capabilities that sit at the intersection of offensive research and defensive operations. The company served government and commercial clients requiring advanced, purpose-built solutions rather than off-the-shelf products.

## Acquisition

In August 2024, Boldend was acquired by SIXGEN, a defense-focused cybersecurity company. The acquisition brought Boldend's technical capabilities and team under SIXGEN's broader mission of delivering specialized cyber solutions to national security clients.

## Why It Mattered

The market for government-grade offensive and defensive tooling is deliberately opaque, but the technical problems it solves — covert capability development, automated exploitation research, counter-intrusion tooling — are among the hardest in security. Boldend built at that level.
