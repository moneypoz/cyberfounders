## Overview

Dark Rhino Security delivers enterprise-grade managed security to organizations that can't afford a full in-house security team. Their model bundles continuous monitoring, threat detection, identity and access management, data loss prevention, and cloud security into a single managed service — backed by up to $1 million in cyber-incident coverage.

## What They're Building

Their flagship offering wraps MSSP services — including SOC monitoring, endpoint protection, DLP, CASB, and IAM — into a subscription model sized for SMBs. The cyber insurance component is core to their value proposition: clients aren't just protected technically, they're protected financially.

## Why It Matters

Small and mid-sized businesses are the fastest-growing ransomware target, yet most can't staff or fund a mature security program. Dark Rhino collapses the cost and complexity barrier while adding insured accountability most MSSPs won't touch.
